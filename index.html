What we do

Design & Build Products

From sketch to store: iOS/Android apps, responsive web, and creator platforms. Tight visuals, fast iteration, real traction.

Operate an Ecosystem

Single sign‑on, shared profiles, and cross‑app entitlements. One account follows the user across everything we ship.

Run the Currency Layer

A USD‑pegged unit and branded app coins. Users buy once, spend anywhere in our network — transparent fees, instant swaps.

How it connects

The LucidCore Stack

Identity — unified login, KYC-ready profiles, badges, and permissions across apps.
Balance — portable wallet with a USD‑pegged unit and per‑app coins (skins) for local experiences.
Commerce — purchases, peer‑to‑peer transfers, in‑app stores, and creator payouts.
Affiliates — tiered referrals that share a % of net fees with users who grow the network.
Observability — live dashboards: revenue, cohort retention, fraud/risk signals, and compliance exports.

Why it matters

  • Users keep value as they move between entertainment, social, and finance.
  • Every app ships faster by reusing identity, wallet, and marketplace components.
  • Creators earn more: tips, shops, wishlists, and referral share — all in one ledger.
